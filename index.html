Who the hell are Red Mess?

Red Mess was formed in Londrina/Brazil in 2014 by Thiago Franzim (Guitar), Douglas Labigalini (Drums) and Lucas Klepa (Bass/Vocals) in Londrina/Brazil with the purpose of playing heavy, groovy and nasty riffs over somehow progressive arrangements. That idea evolved through the years and incorporated a lot of different references along the way, shaping the noticeable authenticity of their music.

After almost a decade of messing around, all of the members moved to Europe and the band is currently based in Berlin.
